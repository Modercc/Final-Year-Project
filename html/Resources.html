Coursera

Founded in 2012 by professors Andrew Ng and Daphne Koller at Stanford University, it is one of the biggest online course providers. After completing the course, students earn a certificate that is accredited and recognized by many employers.

Brilliant

A for-profit company that provides courses and exams in mathematics, physics, quantitative finance and computer science. Courses are explained through innovative, simple approaches, not assuming high prior knowledge of students.

3Blue1Brown

It is a YouTube channel created by Grant Sanderson that got famous for explaining complicated mathematical concepts using interesting animation techniques and insightful analogies. Sanderson is only one of the prominent educators that uses YouTube as a teaching platform to reach their students.

W3Schools

W3Schools is one of the largest and most commonly used websites for web development tutorials and support. It offers a vast range of courses and tutorials among different platforms and tools.
Along with the official Bootstrap 4 website, tutorials on Bootstrap 4 provided by W3Schools could be considered as very concise and structured, and therefore, the most suitable for the beginners.
